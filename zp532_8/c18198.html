Michael Joseph Jackson was born in 1958 in Gary, Indiana to a large working-class family. Michael made his musical debut at six years old alongside his brothers as a member of the Jackson 5. Exhibiting prodigious talent, Michael began singing lead vocals for the group and in 1972, Motown released the first of 1 solo albums. In 1979, following the move to Epic Records, Michael launched his solo career as an adult with the release of Off the Wall followed by 6 additional solo studio albums including Thriller, the best selling album of all time; he won 13 Grammys, and earned himself the title as the King of Pop. Michael Jackson passed away on June 25th, 2009 but to this day is considered the greatest entertainer of all time.
This Spring, supreme will release a collection featuring imagery of Michael Jackson. The collection consists of a Hooded Sweatshirt, I-Shirt, short sleeve Work Shirt and a Skateboard Deck.
